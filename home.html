FAQs

What's the use of the JustAlgoTrades platform?
It is an Algo/API trading platform. We have fully automated intraday trading strategies which we will be executing in your trading account on behalf of you.
What is Algo/Api trading?
Algo trading is a set of predefined rules which are automated using API trading for the execution of trades. Brokers provide an API trading facility using which we can automate enter, exit, cancel and modify of trades. Manual trading has its disadvantage in terms of not executing at the price at which you expect to enter or exit the trade. You can't execute multiple trades in a fraction of a second. But API trading does it all for you.
Can I trust your strategies?
Our strategies have been backtested for over 6 years of intraday data and they have been working consistently year on year with minimal drawdown. So you can trust that the probability of history repeats in the future also. We split the investment into 40+ companies into different intervals of time in a day with strict stop loss for every trade. As the capital is spread across and with stop loss when a trade goes against you, you don't have to worry about going into a large loss. Our strategies have been given results as fewer loss days and more profit days.
Is there any risk involved?
We don't say that there is absolutely no risk. But there is minimal risk and that too for a few days. Let us elaborate. When you make a loss, it's little because of our strict stop loss and capital distribution across multiple company shares. We don't give false promises like our strategies will work 100% of the time. Our probability of success rate is 70%. Means out of 100 days, 30 days will be loss days and 70 days will be profit days. So in case you start getting continuous loss days, do not stop deploying because as per the probability profit days are ahead and these profit days might recover most of the loss and turn you into profit overall.
