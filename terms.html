23. Slippages:

On a live trading day, slippages are normal and we can't do much with that. Though we select only liquid stocks, if the number of shares to exit are more compared to market depth, you will endup exiting at a price which may reduce the profit and we are not responsible for that. On a volatile day or on a day where stock price is volatile, your stop loss will be executed at costlier price and we can't do anything with these scenarios.

24. Circuit hit:

When share of a price hit upper or lower circuit and your position in the stock is in loss, then we can't exit the position untill circuit is opened in case of f&o shares and in case of non f&o shares, circuit will not open during rest of the trading day. In this case, your respective broker will exit the position on auction in the next T+2 days. Because of this, your loss will be higher and we are not responsible for these kind of scenarios.

25. Exit of positions:

We will always try to exit positions @ 3PM. We are not responsible in case of not exiting the position because of internet connectivity or server problem. In that case your respective broker will charge for squareoff from their end and we are not responsible for these charges.

26. Entry of trades:

On a volatile day because of national or global news, we will try to avoid entering because of risk in terms of stop loss getting hit in many of our trades. We are not responsible for not entering the trades on any given day where in we face any internet connectivity issue or our server going down or any glitch in our algorithm code.
